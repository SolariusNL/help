# Changing your password

To change your Framework account password, follow these steps:

1. Log in to your Framework account at [https://framework.solarius.me/login](https://framework.solarius.me/login).
2. Click on your name in the top right corner of the page.
3. Click on **Settings** in the dropdown menu.
4. Navigate to the **Security** tab indicated by the key icon.
5. In the **Password** box, click on the **Change** button.
6. Enter the required information and click on the **Confirm** button.
7. Your password has been changed successfully.

If you have any questions, please contact us through [our support methods](/support). We are happy to help!
