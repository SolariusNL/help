# Infra

Infra is our cloud hosting provider service. It allows you to deploy your applications to the cloud, manage S3 buckets, host databases, and more. Infra is built from the ground up to be secure, reliable, and scalable.

## Features

- **Deploy applications**: Deploy your applications to the cloud with ease. Infra supports a wide range of programming languages and frameworks.
  - Next.js (Server actions supported & edge caching)
  - Node.js
  - Cosmic
  - and more
- **Manage S3 buckets**: Store and retrieve files with ease using S3 buckets. Infra provides a simple and secure way to manage your files.
- **Host databases**: Host your databases in the cloud with Infra. Infra supports a wide range of databases, including MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB.
- **Monitor performance**: Monitor the performance of your applications and databases with Infra's built-in monitoring tools.
- **Scale with ease**: Infra is built to scale with your business. Whether you're a small startup or a large enterprise, Infra can handle your workload.

## Pricing

Infra offers a range of pricing plans to suit your needs. Whether you're a small startup or a large enterprise, Infra has a plan for you. Our pricing is transparent and competitive, with no hidden fees.
